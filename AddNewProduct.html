Learn what to do or not

"id": - id is different Example:- 1,2,3,4,etc, Do not add same number for different product. If you add same number then this app will not show animation and you will get error on your app.

"price": price shoud not be int its double like: 1.00, 22.55, 292.44, 33.9, 01.0, 12.33 etc. and what not to do Example: 11, 33, 033, 424, etc, if your product is only 245 then you need to enter 245.00 that's it.

"image": enter any image url link (for faster experience add uploaded github images from your project.) Note: Image size should be 400x400 px , 500x500px (Recomended),
